What is ABWCI?

Association of Business Women in Commerce & Industry (ABWCI) is an integrated platform dedicated to the economic empowerment of women in business.

As the number of women in business rise globally, it becomes essential that they learn from each other. By building on each other’s strengths, they can move towards achieving collective goals.



ABWCI helps women in business by:


  • Offer them a platform to represent their businesses
  • Connecting them to several relevant opportunities
  • Proving them access to useful resources

Our Mission

ABWCI's mission is to establish an ecosystem to engage, network, identify and leverage market opportunities globally.

Our Mandate

Create a platform to exchange skills, resources, and experiences

Share Knowledge and celebrate successes

Support women in business by boosting an inclusive economy

Connecting women and businesses

Unlock profitability and create a level playing field

Enhance opportunities and build capacities
