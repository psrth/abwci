OUR PURPOSE

ABWCI's purpose is to provide an enabling and nurturing environment for women in business to build a sustainable and better future.

OUR MISSION

ABWCI's mission is to establish a collaborative ecosystem to engage, network, identify and leverage market opportunities globally.
